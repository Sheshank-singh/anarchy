Game Rules

Basic Rules

  • Each team starts with 2 lives
  • 18 questions total (17 regular + 1 major)
  • Regular questions worth 1 point each
  • Points can be used to attack other teams
  • Teams can't be attacked more than twice consecutively

Special Features

  • Major question (18th) grants special powers:
    • Instantly revive your team
    • Instantly eliminate another team
  • Purchase vests for additional protection
  • Real-time chat with team members
  • Game ends when only two teams remain
